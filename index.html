From a Dream to a Sweet Reality

Kumo, meaning 'cloud' in Japanese, was born from a passion for creating the perfect marshmallow. We believe in quality ingredients, small-batch production, and the simple joy that a perfect, fluffy treat can bring. Each marshmallow is handcrafted with care, ensuring a pillowy texture and unforgettable flavor in every bite.

Our journey started in a small home kitchen, and has grown into a mission to share our love for these gourmet sweets with the world. We can't wait for you to try them.
